Our Rules and FAQ

At Pet Paradise, we strive to create a welcoming and safe environment for both our pets and our customers. Below are the guidelines we ask everyone to follow, along with some frequently asked questions to help you with your pet-related needs.

1.Pet Welfare First:
The well-being of our animals is our top priority. Please do not disturb or handle any animals without the assistance of our staff.
2.Hygiene and Cleanliness:
Please use the hand sanitizers provided at the entrance and throughout the store before and after interacting with any pets. This helps us maintain a clean and safe environment for everyone.
3.Supervision of Children:
Children must be supervised at all times. While we encourage learning and interaction, we ask that parents ensure their children are gentle and respectful toward the animals.
4.No Outside Pets Allowed:
For the safety of our store animals and to prevent the spread of diseases, we do not allow outside pets inside the store.
5.Respectful Behavior:
We ask all visitors to be respectful of our staff, other customers, and the animals. Any aggressive or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.
